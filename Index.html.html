WELL-BEING & QUALITY OF LIFE

Health

Health is a state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease. Good health enables individuals to perform their daily activities effectively and live productive lives. Maintaining good health requires balanced nutrition, regular physical activity, adequate rest, and access to proper healthcare services.

Healthy Lifestyle

A healthy lifestyle involves making choices that promote long-term well-being. These choices include eating nutritious food, engaging in regular exercise, avoiding harmful substances, and managing stress effectively. Adopting healthy habits helps prevent chronic diseases and improves overall quality of life.

Mental Well-Being

Mental well-being refers to an individual’s emotional and psychological state. It affects how people think, feel, and behave in daily life. Good mental health enables individuals to cope with stress, build positive relationships, and make sound decisions. Awareness and support are essential in promoting mental well-being in society.
